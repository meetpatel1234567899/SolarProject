Space Exploration

Major Milestones

  • 1957: Sputnik 1, first artificial satellite
  • 1961: Yuri Gagarin, first human in space
  • 1969: Apollo 11, first humans on the Moon
  • 1990: Hubble Space Telescope launched
  • 2012: Curiosity rover lands on Mars

Current Missions

  • International Space Station
  • Mars Perseverance Rover
  • James Webb Space Telescope
  • Parker Solar Probe

Future of Space Exploration

The future of space exploration is exciting, with plans for:

  • Crewed missions to Mars
  • Exploration of potentially habitable moons like Europa and Enceladus
  • Advanced space telescopes to search for exoplanets and signs of life
  • Mining asteroids for resources
