Asteroids and Comets

Asteroids

Asteroids are rocky objects that orbit the Sun, primarily found in the asteroid belt between Mars and Jupiter.

Notable Asteroids:

  • Ceres
  • Vesta
  • Pallas

Comets

Comets are icy bodies that release gas and dust when they get close to the Sun, forming a coma and often a tail.

Famous Comets:

  • Halley's Comet
  • Hale-Bopp
  • Comet NEOWISE
